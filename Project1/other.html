GIANT TUBE WORM

These big ol' lipsticks aren't actually worms and are more related to clams than anything. However, because science is a deliberation of what is being said the most instead of what's right, the name stuck. Despite their brilliant red color, they have never known the sun and spend their days eating bacteria in the darkest depths of the ocean.
